Question: What are the symptoms of depression?
Answer: - Low mood persisting for longer than two weeks
- Dismal outlook and sense of gloom
- Mood lifting with positive experiences but falling with minor disappointments
- Severe depression with persistent low mood throughout the day
- Fluctuating mood during the day (diurnal variation)

Question: How do thoughts influence feelings in depression?
Answer: Thoughts influence feelings in depression by causing individuals to experience negative emotions such as sadness, hopelessness, and despair. Automatic negative thoughts can worsen mood and lead to a cycle of negative thinking and feelings. Challenging unhelpful thoughts and replacing them with balanced thoughts can help improve mood and reduce symptoms of depression. Core beliefs, which are deeply held beliefs about oneself and the world, can also impact thoughts and feelings in depression.
