Question: How can one maintain progress made in managing depression?
Answer: - By continuing to practice the strategies and techniques learned in the workbook
- Setting and working towards goals
- Monitoring thoughts and challenging unhelpful beliefs
- Addressing core beliefs
- Staying healthy and engaging in activities that promote well-being
- Seeking support from mental health professionals if needed
