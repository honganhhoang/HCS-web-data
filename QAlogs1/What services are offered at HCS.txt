Question: How is confidentiality maintained at Health & Counselling Services (HCS)?
Answer: - Health & Counselling shares one unit of confidentiality
- All records within HCS are confidential
- Personal information can only be released outside the centre with written consent
- Exceptions are made in the case of life-threatening emergencies or as required by law and/or public health regulations
