Our organization demonstrates commitment by recognizing the unique contributions of First Nations, Metis, and Inuit Peoples, continually innovating to improve the experience of Indigenous candidates and employees, increasing recruitment outcomes through culturally safe processes, establishing retention infrastructure that supports successful careers, creating a culturally safe workplace that promotes understanding of Indigenous cultures and histories, and expanding partnerships with Indigenous organizations and communities.
