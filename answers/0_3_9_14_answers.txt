1.
- S.A.F.E.R. is a free, time-limited counselling service
- Provides counselling for individuals aged 19 and over who have made a suicide attempt, are currently suicidal, or have suicidal ideation
- Offers support and education for people concerned about the risk of suicide in a significant other or bereaved by a suicide death
- Provides training, education, and professional consultation in the areas of suicide prevention, intervention, and postvention

3.
- S.A.F.E.R. offers up to three appointments with a counsellor to a person concerned about someone who is suicidal.
- Discuss ways to look after yourself.
- S.A.F.E.R. offers individual counselling to individuals who have lost someone to suicide.
- S.A.F.E.R. also offers counsellor-led groups for those ready to work on their grief with others.
