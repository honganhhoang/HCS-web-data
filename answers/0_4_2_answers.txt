Wait times can vary depending on time, location, and level of need. People are seen based on how hurt or sick they are, with the sickest people seen first. Many hospital sites provide 24-hour emergency services, but operating hours may vary. It is recommended to call HealthLink BC at 8-1-1 to verify hours of operation for emergency services and view current wait times.

- UPCCs provide patient-centered care by a team of health care providers.
- They are for people who have an urgent but non-life threatening injury and/or illness and need to be seen by a doctor or nurse practitioner within 12-24 hours.
- UPCCs provide team-based, everyday health care for urgent care needs that do not require emergency attention.
