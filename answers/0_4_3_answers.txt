8.
UPCCs provide access to same-day, urgent, non-emergency health care and are an alternative to visiting an emergency department for non-emergency issues. Patients who require medical attention within 12 to 24 hours for minor injuries, sprains, strains, infections, and cuts can visit a UPCC.

9.
There are Urgent and Primary Care Centres available in many communities across British Columbia as part of the Province’s Primary Care Strategy. UPCCs are regularly being launched across the province. Keep checking the Government of BC website for more updates.
