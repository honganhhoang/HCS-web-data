The AMI is a free service designed to enhance social experience and academic success. Participants are matched with a trained SFU student mentor and meet regularly to explore areas of interest or concern. Questionnaires may be completed throughout involvement, and participation is not mandatory. If interested, current or incoming SFU students diagnosed with autism can apply by filling out a form.

5.
The AMI program starts every September (fall term) and runs until the following May (end of spring term).

6.
On average, you will meet one-on-one with your mentor about once per week, for 1-2 hours at a time.

7.
- Improving academic skills
- Communication and interpersonal skills
- Finding out about campus activities and resources
- Connecting and talking about life at school

8.
