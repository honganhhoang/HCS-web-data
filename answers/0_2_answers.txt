Health & Counselling operates two medical clinics located on the Burnaby and Vancouver campuses with services in-person or by phone/virtual for all students.

2.
The medical clinics are located on the Burnaby and Vancouver campuses.

3.
Students can book appointments for medical services by calling the Health & Counselling clinics on the Burnaby or Vancouver campuses, or by booking online. Appointments can be made for in-person or by phone/virtual consultations.

4.
Same-day access is limited.

5.
Students should expect to need to obtain additional medical documentation. They are encouraged to connect with their care providers or reach out to Health & Counselling Services for care and consultation if they need it.

6.
Students can apply for BC Medical Services Plan (BC MSP) by visiting SFU's Medical Insurance page.

7.
- Health & Counselling shares one unit of confidentiality
- All records within HCS are confidential
- Personal information can only be released outside the centre with written consent
- Exceptions are made in the case of life-threatening emergencies or as required by law and/or public health regulations
