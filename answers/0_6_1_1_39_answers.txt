ICBC demonstrates its commitment by having a Special Program designation from the BC Human Rights Commissioner to preferentially target and hire self-identified Indigenous people, aiming for greater Indigenous employee representation. The organization actively engages in initiatives for both customers and employees to learn, grow, and address the disadvantages faced by Indigenous people, fostering a more inclusive and diverse workplace. Additionally, ICBC has created specific Indigenous roles, maintains an Indigenous Talent Community to connect Indigenous applicants with job vacancies, offers practicum opportunities for Indigenous students, and has an Indigenous Employee Resource Group that facilitates connection and inclusive activities.
