1.
- The purpose of the trauma seminar is to help people learn about different types of trauma and how they can affect individuals.
- The seminar aims to help participants understand common symptoms of trauma and equip them with basic self-help strategies.
- It also provides information on where to access additional support for those struggling after experiencing trauma.

2.
- Trauma can affect individuals by causing emotional distress and mental health issues.
- Trauma can lead to symptoms such as anxiety, depression, and post-traumatic stress disorder.
- Trauma can impact relationships, daily functioning, and overall well-being.
- Trauma can manifest physically through symptoms such as headaches, stomach issues, and fatigue.

3.
- Common symptoms of trauma may include:
- Emotional symptoms such as anxiety, depression, and mood swings
- Physical symptoms such as headaches, fatigue, and stomach issues
- Cognitive symptoms such as memory problems, difficulty concentrating, and racing thoughts
- Behavioral symptoms such as irritability, social withdrawal, and self-destructive behaviors
