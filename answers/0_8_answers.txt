Faculty members can share health and well-being information with students at SFU through in-class activities, teaching practices, content sharing, and using presentation slide decks provided by SFU Health Promotion. They can also introduce tools and topics related to well-being during class time, facilitate quick class check-in activities, and share self-guided mindfulness resources. Additionally, they can create inclusive classrooms by supporting the declaration of self-identified pronouns and fostering social connections among students.
