11.
The Bouncing Forward Resilience Course is a free, CCR-credited online Canvas course that provides strategies for students to build their personal resilience and improve their well-being. It can be shared with students in syllabi, lecture slides, or announcements, and SFU Health Promotion can also present the content to classes.

12.
To create celebratory research poster sessions for students to share their projects, you can follow these steps:

1. Design a poster session that meets educational goals.
2. Include non-graded awards to celebrate students' accomplishments.
3. Encourage creativity in the conceptual development of projects.
4. Provide opportunities for students to showcase their work in a supportive environment.
