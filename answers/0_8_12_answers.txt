3.
Flexible deadlines for assignments can offer compassion and empowerment to students, particularly when so much is out of their control. It allows students to manage their time effectively, reduces stress and anxiety, and accommodates individual circumstances or challenges they may be facing. This flexibility can also help students maintain a sense of control over their learning, leading to increased motivation, engagement, and overall well-being.
