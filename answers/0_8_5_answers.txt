The Global Student Centre supports student well-being at SFU by fostering inclusivity, social connectedness, and community. 

Students feel happier, study more effectively, and are encouraged to spend more time at school and socialize at the Global Student Centre. 

The Centre provides a well-designed space that enhances accessibility and functionality for individual study, group work, presentations, and research conferences. 

SFU Health Promotion was consulted during the planning stages for the FHS Student Commons to delineate individual study space versus collaborative space, resulting in a space that is well-utilized by FHS students.
