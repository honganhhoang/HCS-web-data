1.
SFU is committed to creating and maintaining a healthy, safe, and respectful learning, teaching, research, and work environment in which bullying and harassment are neither acceptable nor tolerated.

2.
- Support and services are available to anyone impacted by bullying and harassment at SFU.
- You can meet with the Anti-Bullying Specialist who can support you in submitting a report or you can submit a confidential inquiry through the Bullying & Harassment Report Webform.
- All SFU students, staff, and faculty have a right to be treated with respect and dignity.
- You can connect with our Anti-Bullying Specialist if you have experienced/observed or are experiencing bullying and harassment.
- To learn more about respectful working and learning environments, request learning opportunities for your team, and access toolkits.

3.
Bullying and harassment at SFU includes any inappropriate conduct or comment by a person towards a member of the University Community that would cause the member to be humiliated or intimidated.

4.
Reporting incidents of bullying and harassment is not a requirement to access support at SFU.

- Submit a report through the Bullying & Harassment Report Webform
