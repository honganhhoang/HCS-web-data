The Indigenous University Preparation Pathway (IUPP) is a program that welcomes First Nations, Métis, and Inuit students to a university learning environment that affirms and integrates Indigenous knowledge and perspectives.

2.
First Nations, Métis, and Inuit students

3.
The purpose of the IUPP at Simon Fraser University is to welcome First Nations, Métis, and Inuit students to a university learning environment that affirms and integrates Indigenous knowledge and perspectives, and to support them as they explore university alongside Elders, mentors, tutors, and instructors.

4.
Elders, mentors, tutors, and instructors

5.
Indigenous knowledge and perspectives are integrated into the IUPP through affirming and incorporating Indigenous ways of knowing, cultural practices, languages, histories, and worldviews.
