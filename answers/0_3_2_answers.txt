2.
Health & Counselling offers free, confidential, time-limited counselling support to all registered SFU and FIC students, from any campus. The counselling team provides initial consultation and assessment, information and referral, workshops, crisis support, safety planning, and individual and group counselling to address various concerns. Counselling services are provided by Registered Clinical Counsellors, Registered Psychologists, and their professional student-trainees. There are also specialized counselling options available.

3.
Three to four appointments.

4.
- You can express your preference for counsellor gender, language, background, and more.
- Complete a short questionnaire regarding your concerns and request for support.
- The mental health clinician will ask questions to clarify what you would like help with and will review your questionnaire with you.
- Identify a goal and an action plan of support including resources to best accommodate your concerns.
- If referred to another resource outside of Health & Counselling, the clinician will provide support and assist in accessing appropriate community resources and referrals.

5.

11.
My SSP is a service contracted by the university. SFU students can access this service by calling or chatting using the app, or arranging for short-term counselling with a My SSP counsellor by appointment.
