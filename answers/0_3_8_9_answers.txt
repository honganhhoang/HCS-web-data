1.
To register for the 8-week counselling group with a disability focus for students with an Autism diagnosis, you can contact your CAL disability advisor and indicate your interest in participating in the group. Your advisor will then forward you an email referral with instructions on how to book an intake appointment with the group facilitator. During a 30-minute group screening meeting, the facilitator will discuss whether the group would be a good fit for you. Space in the groups is limited, so once the group is filled, it will be closed for the semester, and a waitlist will be created for future offerings.

2.
The topics discussed in the counselling group for autistic students at SFU include struggles currently experienced by group members, coping strategies, navigating post-secondary education, and a variety of other topics brought by participants.

4.
