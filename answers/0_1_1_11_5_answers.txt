- Awareness, understanding, and knowledge of mental health issues can help individuals move towards achieving emotional well-being by providing information on symptoms and treatments for different mental health conditions.
- It can help individuals identify when they or someone they know may be in distress and in need of support.
- Accessing professional assistance through counseling, coaching, and e-courses can help individuals and their family members resolve personal and work-related problems that affect their well-being.
- Utilizing resources and support services provided by organizations such as Wellness Together Canada, Mood Disorders Association of BC, Canadian Mental Health Association, BC Division, Employee and Family Assistance Program (EFAP), BC Partners for Mental Health and Addictions Information, Ministry of Mental Health and Addictions, HealthLink BC, and BC Bereavement Helpline can contribute to improving emotional well-being.
