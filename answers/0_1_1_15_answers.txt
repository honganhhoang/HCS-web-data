1.
The purpose of the Office of Student Support, Rights & Responsibilities at SFU is to provide resources for students, faculty, and staff, to ensure appropriate responses and level of supports are provided to students, to help students better understand their responsibilities and rights as members of the SFU community, and to create a safe and respectful learning environment.
