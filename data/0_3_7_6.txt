Out On Campus is a department of the Simon Fraser Student Society dedicated to supporting SFU’s LGBTQIA2S+ (Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer or Questioning, Intersex, Aromantic/Asexual, 2-Spirit and more) students and allies. All are welcome to use our space and access our services. We are funded by the SFSS and the Graduate Student Society and we are committed to building a more just and open world for LGBTQIA2S+ people of all intersections and walks of life, so anyone who shares this vision or would like to learn more about it is welcome to drop by!
