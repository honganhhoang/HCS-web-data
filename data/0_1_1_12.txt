 

Excerpts from the 2017-2018 Annual Report on Student Discipline Matters.

Types of Incidents: 

Outcomes:   Informal resolutions (39%), apologies (29%), formal reprimands (18%), are the most common resolutions.

Excerpts from the 2016-2017 Annual Report on Student Discipline Matters.

Types of Incidents: 

Outcomes:  Informal resolutions (44%), apology letters (32%) and formal reprimands (12%) are the most common resolutions

Excerpts from the 2015-2016 Annual Report on Student Discipline Matters.

Types of Incidents: 

Outcomes:  Informal Resolution (48% of cases) and Formal Reprimand (36% of cases) continue to be the most common resolutions. 

Excerpts from the 2014-2015 Annual Report on Student Discipline Matters.

Types of Incidents:

Outcomes:  Formal Reprimands (55% of cases) and Informal Resolutions (27% of cases) are the most common resolution.

Excerpts from the 2013-2014 Annual Report on Student Discipline Matters

Types of Incidents: 

Outcomes: Formal Reprimands (47% of cases) and Informal Resolutions (41% of cases) are the most common resolution; followed by Recommendations to seek professional assistance (6% of cases) and Apology Letters (6% of cases).
