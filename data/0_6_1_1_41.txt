The Indigenous University Preparation Pathway (IUPP) welcomes First Nations, Métis, and Inuit students to a university learning environment that affirms and integrates Indigenous knowledge and perspectives. You will join a cohort of like-minded Indigenous students and explore university together with Elders, mentors, tutors, and instructors.
