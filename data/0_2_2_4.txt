Planning to spend some time at Wreck Beach? Naked or clothed, we’ll be there for you! For your standard STI testing, pop by and let us help you with your sexual health. You can come see us at the parking lot by Trail 7. Drop-in only. No appointments. 

HIM in Surrey Health Centre provides STI/HIV testing and treatment services for GBQ men and gender diverse people on weekly basis. This location doesn’t offer appointments, clients can just do walk-in to see a nurse. We require all attendees to read our Code of Conduct.
