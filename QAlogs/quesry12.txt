What is the background and experience of the facilitator, Tricia-Kay Williams?
Tricia-Kay Williams has a BA degree in Psychology from York University in Toronto, ON, and an MA degree in Counselling Psychology from Adler University in Vancouver, BC. She has extensive experience counseling individuals and families, treating trauma, anxiety, transitional/career, and relational issues. Tricia is also an advocate for anti-racism affecting BIPOC individuals and consults with organizations to foster a more anti-racist approach.
