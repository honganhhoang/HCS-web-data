How can SFU students access the health and counselling services on campus?
SFU students can access health and counselling services on campus by visiting the student health clinics on Burnaby and Vancouver campus, booking appointments with doctors and nurses, and exploring resources and support options available. Services are provided in-person, virtually, or by phone at no cost to SFU students.
